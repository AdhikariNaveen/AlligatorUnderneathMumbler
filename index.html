Both Theory and Meto combine the alternative metal, nu metal,and rap rock sound with influences and elements from hip hop, alternative rock,and electronica, utilizing programming and synthesizers. William Ruhlmann from AllMusic regarded it as "a Johnny-come-lately to an already overdone musical style,"whereas Rolling Stone described their song "Breaking the Habit" as "risky, beautiful art".
